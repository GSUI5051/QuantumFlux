Story

You got a small contained black hole for your birthday. Just what you always wanted.
You go out and buy a quantum field interaction device and you start making virtual electron positron pairs next to the black hole.
Every now and then the black hole will excite a pair of virtual particles into reality, and consume the positron.
You are now left with one more electron than you started with, and using a small vaccum chamber, you are keeping it safe.
When you get enough particles, you can get more qfids, and make even more particles!
